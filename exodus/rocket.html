The year is 2041. Earth is no longer habitable for human life.

WHAT HAPPENED TO THE EARTH

In March 2034, an unrevealed group of hackers from Russia called Xakeprep hacked into the Indian database systems. This caused a a huge power surge, which in turn led to famines, thefts and a 61% increase in crime rate in the country. On May 2035, the Latvian army invaded Russia due to an unsolved land issue. This inflicted chaos and tension all over world, leading to stockpiling of weapons in 107 countries. By February 2036, 62 countries sided with Bulgaria, and 59 countries sided Turkey, bringing about a total cost of 925.14 trillion dolars and an estimated causalty of 7.2 billion people. On December 2040, Indonesia signed an agreement with Brazil, bringing an end to the war. However, due to the massive amount of destruction, pollution and loss of resources, Earth was no longer feasible to live in, forcing humans to evacuate the planet and look for alternate living sites.

AUSTERE MAJOR

Austere Major is a red, terestrial planet located in the Large Magellanic Cloud, about a 163,000 lightyears from the Earth. Its extremely rich and iron, hydrogen and nitrogen. Its atmosphere has an oxygen content of 16%, a nitrogen content of 82% and a carbon content of 1.7%. It has a gravity of 10.5 meters per square second and an average temperature of 3.2 degrees celcius. It has a surface area of 91.4 million square kilometers, and 47% of its surface is covered by ice. The first humans arrived on Austere Major on November 2040. As of May 2041, Austere Major has a total population of 1.05 billion. The only currency used is the Amarthan, which values the same as 10.96 Indian Rupees. The only source of entertainment so far in the planet is a cinema theatre named Ionox located about 24 kilometers from civilization.
