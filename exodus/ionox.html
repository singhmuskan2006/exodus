Welcome to Ionox


We at Ionox are here to keep you entertained and distracted from the apocalypse. We present to you 5 new movies made by us. The other 5 are your all-time favourites which we are pretty sure all of you miss.

_

ORIGINALS

_

Space Man: Far From Home

A documentary about an astronaut who decides to explore space but gets lost. He then meets a mysterious astronaut who offers to help him.

Moon Girls

Cady moves to the moon and is surprised to find how different people in the moon are compared to her home planet. She befriends a group of girls called "Carbon" but things get heated up as she slowly gains popularity.

Insidious: The Last Galaxy

A parapsychologist is terrorized by an alien in the Whirlpool Galaxy, and she is forced to return back to her home galaxy. She has to fight the alien to protect the galaxy.

Pirates of The Constellation

A rover mechanic joins forces with Captian Jack Salvina to set free the love of his life from the hands of the captain of the Dark Jewel.

La La Land

A musical of two people, Mia and Sebastian, two aspiring artists who fall in love while pursuing their dreams.. However, due to the recent apocalypse, the dreams they worked so hard for destroy their love.

_

THROWBACKS

_

Inception

Dom Cobb is a thief with the rare ability to enter people's dreams and steal their secrets from their subconscious. His skill has made him a hot commodity in the world of corporate espionage, but has also cost him everything he loves. Cobb gets a chance at redemption when he is offered a seemingly impossible task: Plant an idea in someone's mind. If he succeeds, it will be the perfect crime, but a dangerous enemy anticipates Cobb's every move.

Spirited Away

10-year-old Chihiro and her parents stumble upon a seemingly abandoned amusement park. After her mother and father are turned into giant pigs, Chihiro meets the mysterious Haku, who explains that the park is a resort for supernatural beings who need a break from their time spent in the earthly realm, and that she must work there to free herself and her parents.

Titanic

Jack Dawson wins a chance to board the Titanic. There, he meets Rose who is set to be married and they fall in love.

Star Wars

Princess Leia is held hostage by the evil forces of the Galactic Empire in their effort to take over the galaxy. Venturesome Luke Skywalker and dashing Captain Han Solo team together with the lovable robotic duo, R2-D2 and C-3PO, to rescue the beautiful Princess Leia and restore justice in the galaxy.

Shutter Island

A U.S. Marshal investigates the disappearance of a murderer who escaped from a hospital for the criminally insane. Soon, within the dark purgatory's thick stone walls, indescribable nuances of medical malpractice suggest that the institution's cryptic senior psychiatrists must be hiding something.
